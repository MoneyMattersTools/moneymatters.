Why we exist

Financial planning shouldn’t require a six-figure salary or a finance degree.

Professional planners charge hundreds to thousands per session. Most online tools either oversimplify your situation, hide the math behind their numbers, or harvest your data to push financial products you didn’t ask for.

MoneyMatters was built to fix this — to put serious, transparent financial tools in the hands of people who want to actually understand and control their money.

What makes us different

You own it.

Pay once, and the tool is yours forever. No subscriptions, no rented access, no features getting paywalled later.

Transparent math.

Every formula is visible and editable. Adapt the tool to your situation, learn how the numbers work, and trust the output because you can see how it was calculated.

Vetted, not sold.

Every advisor in our network is independently vetted for CFP® certification, fee-only compensation, and fiduciary duty. We don't sell your data.

Real depth.

How we make money (and why it doesn't compromise you)

MoneyMatters is free to use because we're paid by the advisors in our network — not by you. When you choose to connect with an advisor, we may receive a referral fee. That fee never affects which advisor you're matched with: every advisor in our network is independently vetted for CFP® certification, fee-only compensation, and fiduciary duty, full stop. We don't get paid more for pushing you toward any one advisor, product, or outcome. Your data is only shared with an advisor when you explicitly choose to connect — never by default, never sold to anyone else.

Frequently asked questions

Why do I need to make a copy of the sheet?

Your copy is private and yours alone. The master template stays clean for everyone, and you can edit, save, and revisit your version anytime — without affecting anyone else.

Can I share my copy with my spouse or partner?

Yes. Once you have your copy, you can share it with anyone in your household just like any Google Sheet.

Do you offer refunds?

If a tool isn’t what you expected, contact us within 14 days for a full refund.

Will the tools get updates?

Yes. We periodically improve the tools and notify buyers of significant updates. You can request the updated version anytime.

Is this financial advice?

No. MoneyMatters tools are educational and for your personal planning. For decisions with major consequences, please consult a licensed financial professional.

How is this different from apps like Mint or YNAB?

Those are subscription apps with locked-down features and your data in their hands. Our tools are spreadsheets you fully own and can adapt — no recurring fees, no lock-in.

Built on real principles

Our tools are grounded in widely-accepted personal finance frameworks: zero-based budgeting, the 4% safe withdrawal rule for retirement, compound-interest modeling for long-term planning, and standard net-worth accounting.

Where we make assumptions (like return rates or inflation), we tell you what they are and let you adjust them. No black boxes.
