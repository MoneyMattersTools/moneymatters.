Frequently asked questions

What's actually free?

The Financial Health Score and Net Worth diagnostics, and the Budget, Retirement, and Investment tools — all free, no signup wall to try them. Creating an account (via a magic-link email, no password) saves your results and unlocks the community and advisor matching.

What do I get for $5/month?

Advanced versions of the tools, new tools as we release them, explainer and how-to videos, webinar access, and advisor Q&A sessions. One subscription, everything included — no per-tool pricing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. It's a monthly subscription with no lock-in — cancel whenever you want and keep access through the end of your billing period.

Is this financial advice?

No. MoneyMatters tools are educational, for your own planning. For decisions with major consequences, connect with one of our vetted advisors or another licensed financial professional.

How do you actually vet advisors?

Every advisor in our network is confirmed CFP®-certified, fee-only (no commissions), and held to a fiduciary standard before they're allowed in front of any user. See how we make money for the full picture.

How is this different from apps like Mint or YNAB?

Those are subscription apps built around linking your bank accounts and locking features behind tiers. Our tools run on the numbers you choose to enter, every formula is visible, and going deeper — advanced tools or a real advisor — is your call, not a paywall you hit by accident.

Built on real principles

Our tools are grounded in widely-accepted personal finance frameworks: zero-based budgeting, the 4% safe withdrawal rule for retirement, compound-interest modeling for long-term planning, and standard net-worth accounting.

Where we make assumptions (like return rates or inflation), we tell you what they are and let you adjust them. No black boxes.
